I am an assistant professor in computer science at the Departamento de Ciência da Computação (CIC) at Universidade de Brasília (UnB), Brasília-DF, Brazil. I earned my Ph.D. degree in computer science and computational mathematics from the Instituto de Ciências Matemáticas e de Computação (ICMC) at Universidade de São Paulo, São Carlos (USP São Carlos). From 2014 to 2015, I accomplished a one-year period as a visiting research scholar at University of California, Davis (UC Davis), California, United States of America. I received my undergraduate and my Master's degrees in Computer Science from the Universidade Federal de Uberlândia (UFU). From 2016 to 2017 I worked as a temporary professor at the Departamento de Ciência da Computação (DCC) at Universidade Federal de Lavras (UFLA). My research interests comprise data visualization, visual data mining, machine learning, computer vision and natural language processing.

My Master's dissertation was awarded the 1st place in the workshop of theses and dissertations of XXV Conference on Graphics, Patterns and Images (SIBGRAPI 2012).

I am one of the coaches of UnB teams in International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) and in Maratona SBC de Programação. I am the coordinator of the extension project Maratona de Programação at Universidade de Brasília.

You can explore my research activities at the Grupo de Visualização e Inteligência Computacional (GVIC) GitLab. I am also a member of the research groups at University of Brasilia: Laboratório de Raciocínio Automatizado (LARA) and Laboratório de Imagens, Sinais e Acústica (LISA).

I am a member of the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC) and IEEE.
